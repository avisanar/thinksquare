ThinkSquare Technologies currently offers internship program for talented students. Please find the details of our intership program below.

Program Structure :

We involve students in Training and Live projects to get the hands on experience on industrial problems which help them to correlate and understand the latest trends in technologies.

Minimum hours expected:

Minimum of 10 hours per week which includes training, assessment and mentorship discussion

How long:

We will hire the interns as full time employees based on the performance demonstrated by the them during their internship period.

Fee:

We expect only your time and dedication

Salary:

Will be decided based on the performance and the terms of employment post the internship.

Eligibility:

  • Current students pursing their engineering degree B.E/B.Tech (2nd Year Onwards).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
  • Good attitude and aggressive to work as a team and deliver.
  • Your willingness to be flexible at work and work in shifts.
  • Ability to work successfully to meet tight schedules and deadlines. Client first attitude.
